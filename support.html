Contact & Data Deletion

We will delete all of your data upon request. You can either delete your account directly in the app (which permanently deletes all associated data), or email us asking for deletion.

Option 1 — Delete your account in the app (instant)

  1. Open the app and go to Profile → Settings.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. This permanently deletes your account and all data.

Once confirmed, your data is removed. This action can’t be undone.

Option 2 — Email us to request deletion

  1. Email us from the address linked to your account.
  2. Say you want your data deleted (a simple request is enough).
  3. We’ll process the request and confirm once the deletion is complete.
